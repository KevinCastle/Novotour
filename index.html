Tours and Vineyards
Chile is not just Santiago, here you can do a lot of things!
Viña del Mar / Valparaiso
Beach, history and wine
Enjoy the great experience of visiting valparaiso, a humankind's heritage city just beside of Viña del Mar the "Garden City".
Ski Center
Ski, montains and snow
One of the best ski resorts in South America just one hour from Santiago where you can put in practice your snowboard skills.
Embalse el Yeso
Lagoons, hot springs and Montains
Through Montains and waterfalls, enjoy the biggest reservoir located in andes montains.
Casablanca Valley
Visit the most known Vineyards
Visit the vineyards of the most producers of white wine in Chile and learn about the viticulture of the Casablanca Valley.

Settled in the South of Santiago is two symbolic vineyards, Concha y Toro and just 30 minutes away is Santa Rita Vineyard.

Great landscapes and red wine
An excellent travel with beautiful landscapes where you will enjoy one of the best red win production.
